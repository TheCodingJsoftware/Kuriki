Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-09-24

1. Data Collection and Storage

  • Kuriki does not require user accounts, and no personal data is collected.
  • Lesson plans created by users are stored locally in their browser.
  • Users have the option to publish lesson plans, making them publicly accessible.
  • When publishing a lesson plan, an author name (as provided by the user) will be displayed publicly.

2. User-Generated Content

  • Users can add resource links to their lesson plans.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring that any URLs shared are safe and appropriate.
  • Kuriki does not verify, moderate, or endorse shared links.

3. Data Security

  • Data is not encrypted.
  • Users should not share any sensitive, private, or personally identifiable information.
  • There is no guarantee of data protection or recovery.

4. Community and Responsibility

  • Users should not post harmful, hateful, illegal, or sexually explicit content.
  • Published lesson plans are public, and other users may reuse or republish them.
  • If users do not want their content to be republished, they should not publish it.
  • The owner and maintainer take no responsibility for any issues or damages resulting from the use of this service.

5. Changes to this Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Kuriki constitutes acceptance of any changes.
